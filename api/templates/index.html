Project Summary


Anti-Civilian Violence Data Analysis

The war in Ukraine has been at the forefront of global news for the past few months. Many news outlets have claimed that human rights abuses have been occurring in the form of violence against civilians. We have visualised the data gathered by the Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project (ACLED) and displayed the data through maps and plots to analyse, compare, and highlight the implications of such conflict event types. We have also visualised violence against civilians data across regions and globally to compare and essentially we wished to answer one question: is this type of violence in Ukraine an exceptional case?
